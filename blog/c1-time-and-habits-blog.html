SETTING UP FOR SUCCESS

Not counting on myself to always be motivated has really worked for me. For example, I get all my meals delivered, and I have a personal trainer. That way I don't need to worry about cooking healthy (the healthy food just turns up) and I don't have to worry about always being motivated to push myself with exercise as I have the trainer to help me! The same will apply to study and development. I would very much like to continue starting small with things, and just keeping them up. Also, meditation! I suffer from anxiety and I think it would really help.
