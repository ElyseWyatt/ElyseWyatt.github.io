7. Meditation process.

PROCESS OVER PRODUCT

This week we had to practice 'Process over Product' which is harder than it sounds! It felt like I didn't even understand the process, let alone the product. And I kept putting too much effort into things without moving on. But in the end I tried not to think of all the things I wanted my website to do, and just moved on to complete the tasks. Being reminded to not spend too much time on things was great.

CHAPTER TWO OF SEARCH INSIDE YOURSELF

Before reading chapter two, I knew a little about meditation and the benefits it creates. From reading the chapter, I learnt that we can invite things to stop distracting us. I also learnt that you can practice meditation in any position. Two things really stood out to me - 1. meditation is great for learning to deal with distractions and thoughts in a positive way (by not reacting immediately to anything), and 2. by practicing meditation, and becoming happier, you can actually help yourself be less likely to get sick.
