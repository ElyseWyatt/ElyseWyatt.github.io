EMPATHIC LISTENING

I have tried empathetic listening and it is amazing. People often feel cared about even more when you ask them about their feelings, rather than just ask them questions. Especially if you ask in a non-obtrusive way.

LOVING KINDNESS MEDITATION

The Loving Kindness Meditation is an extremely helpful exercise to complete when feeling annoyed at someone. I have completed this in the past without even realising I was doing it. The weirdest thing is, it really works. Its very hard to stay annoyed at someone when you very logically say "that person just wants to be happy, and they believe their actions will bring them happiness in some way."
