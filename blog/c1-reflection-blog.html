1. My first reflections.

MY EXPERIENCE SO FAR

The DBC/EDA experience really resonates with me, and I've learnt or been reminded of a few great things while watching the video. Namely, we are all in this together and need to have a hacker mindset. Also, it's up to us to get out what we put in. My first impressions are good. I really like a lot of what Shereef says, and some of it I didn't actually know! I love the type of culture Shereef talks about. I thrive in collaborative environments, and learning by doing is the way I learn best. Super excited!

MOVING FORWARD

It was good to be reminded that this is not an off the shelf product or service. He is right when he talks about our mindsets often being conditioned to expect that. I think I felt like that when I was freaking out in the first couple of hours, and it's a good prompt to not think like that. I'm very excited about EDA! Looking forward to doing a lot more group activities. I'm nervous about the sheer amount of work there is to do. But I have faith it will be okay.
